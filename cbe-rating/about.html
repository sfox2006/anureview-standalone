About ANRevU

Why this project exists.

ANRevU is a student-focused project built to correct the information asymmetries that everyday students go through so that everyone can know what an academic or a course is actually like.

It gives students a place to share honest experiences, compare courses and teaching styles, and make better-informed choices before they commit their time, money and energy to a class.

If you are a student from another university, reach out at ...@gmail.com to gain access to the code and build it for your university, or if you are just looking to collaborate.
